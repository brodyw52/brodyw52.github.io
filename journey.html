journey

From selling beats online at 14 to running eBay flips for profit, I learned early on that I love building from zero. This entrepreneurial spirit was forged alongside a rigorous background in classical piano (ABRSM 8+), where I learned that high-level performance requires obsessive discipline.

Entering college as a varsity athlete (Emory Oxford NJCAA D3), I was sidelined sophomore year after a serious ACL injury. This adversity forced downtime and I channelled my energy into AI, teaching myself growth engineering, scraping, system orchestration and much more.

I fell in love with this work due to how well it fits my strengths in creativity, execution and obsession with data. I love owning outcomes; and in this field, if you drive results, the data proves it.

Today, I've organized my life around this passion. I'm active in hackathons, entrepreneurship clubs and work in VC. Exposure to hundreds of startups has sharpened my judgement. Most ideas are noise, and even fewer successfully execute.

Through my experience, I believe that growth is no longer a volume game; it's an engineering challenge. The future is programmatic, hyper targeted, personalized outreach. I am focused on being at the forefront of this inevitable future, joining or building high growth teams that invent the next generation of acquisition engines.
